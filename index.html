Intrasoft International is the European leader in delivering state of the art Customs technologies. From 2015 to 2018 i was involved in the two of the largest scale projects for Customs Systems Development for European Commission (department of Taxation and Customs Union) and Customs Systems Development for the Association of Southeast Asian Nations.
